/// FORMATION & OPERATIONAL HISTORYADDENDA 1312–1314 // VOLUME 7 OF AN ONGOING AND DEEPLY UPSETTING SERIES

Formation: Formed in the late 2010s, ostensibly as a rapid-response unit to growing cannibal invasions — which is a sentence that required three separate sign-offs before this document could be printed. Founded by Rickelicius, whose background information has been removed from every accessible database on Earth, which the UNSC finds extremely suspicious and Rickelicius apparently finds extremely funny. Origins trace to a private counter-terrorist mercenary cell active from approximately late 2012. This cell conducted legitimate Middle East operations and counter-piracy work in the Gulf of Oman, which was apparently the last time any of them did anything a government could publicly endorse. Irrational Things is confirmed among the founding members of this cell, which raises the question of whether his network capability was present then or developed later. The UNSC has not been able to investigate this question to a satisfactory conclusion, possibly because every investigation into it encounters network anomalies. Analysts have stopped noting this as a coincidence.

The Island Deployment (First Confirmed Sighting): A remote forested island. The C.U.N.T.S. either systematically genocided a cannibal population or destroyed an ancient device causing cannibal outbreaks. The UNSC's investigation into which one it was has produced two competing 400-page reports that contradict each other on every factual point. Both agree that there were a lot of casualties and that the C.U.N.T.S. seemed fine with this. Seven communications were subsequently sent to Rickelicius. All seven were left on read. The read receipts remain the most demoralising documents in the UNSC archive.

The Welsh Incident — RAF Mona: The C.U.N.T.S. assaulted a British Army research base using advanced weaponry and what witnesses describe as weapons made of bone. This is the incident that introduced the world to Instant Dominance. Base footage was largely destroyed, but the surviving frames show something that three senior analysts refused to describe on record and one described only as "no." The British government officially has no comment. They have had no comment for several years now. The UNSC has drafted fourteen letters pointing out that "no comment" does not make a stolen time machine go away. The British government has had no comment about the letters.

Vietnam (Circa 1968): A video emerged of an American combat unit comprised entirely of C.U.N.T.S. members committing what observers described as "multiple atrocities," though the term feels inadequate here. A member whose personnel file has since been restructured called in an F4 napalm strike on a friendly platoon. The C.U.N.T.S. appear to have fought for both sides at different points during this war. When asked by field analysts how this was possible, the question was not answered, because the field analysts had already fled. Grimm was observed during the Tet Offensive deploying weaponry that simply did not exist in 1968. This is consistent with having a time machine. It is not, however, consistent with any reasonable explanation for their behaviour otherwise.

The Wild West (Unconfirmed, Circa 1899): Multiple credible sources. A single photograph exists showing a group portrait consistent with five C.U.N.T.S. members in New Hannover and West Elizabeth circa 1899. Everyone in the photograph looks entirely comfortable. Everyone is dressed appropriately for the period. Nobody looks like they have any concerns whatsoever, which is the concern. Bonelike artefacts were subsequently recovered from the region. Their objective remains unknown. The leading theory is that they were "just having a look round," which makes everything about it worse.

Washington DC — The Dollar Flu (Anomalous): Rickelicius, Grimm, and Phoenix operated as SHD agents and successfully saved Washington DC from collapse during the Dollar Flu outbreak. No buildings were destroyed. No one died who wasn't supposed to die. No artefacts were recovered. No chaos occurred. The C.U.N.T.S. left quietly. UNSC analysts have been attempting to explain this for two years and have produced nothing. The current leading theory — documented in an official intelligence report — is that it was an accident. A secondary theory, not included in official documentation, is that they were bored.

The Payday Affiliation: Several members spent a period affiliated with the Payday criminal crew. What followed was the largest single crime wave in American history. Banks. Armoured vehicles. FBI safehouses. At one point, a train. Estimated total value acquired: in excess of $20 billion. The affiliation ended, apparently, when there was nothing left in the operational area worth stealing. The FBI has described this period as "the worst weeks of our institutional existence." This is the same FBI whose safehouse was broken into specifically to eliminate a witness. The witness elimination is not in the top five worst things that happened to them during this period.

The Monster Hunter Operation (Ongoing — 5+ Years): Independent of all other documented C.U.N.T.S. activity, and running concurrently with most of it, Rickelicius has maintained an active Monster Hunter operation for what UNSC analysts have now confirmed is a period exceeding five years. This is, for reference, longer than the Payday affiliation, longer than the DedSec embedding period, and significantly longer than any individual geopolitical crisis the C.U.N.T.S. have been implicated in. It predates HighLord's recruitment. It predates several incidents in this file. It is the longest single continuous activity in the entire C.U.N.T.S. operational history. Rickelicius hunts monsters. Large ones. Across multiple ecosystems. With what available intelligence suggests is an extremely well-optimised build developed over years of iterative refinement and testing. The ecological impact of sustained high-level monster hunting over a five-year period has been flagged by three separate environmental divisions. None of them have received a response from Rickelicius. None of them expected one.

Super Earth / HighLord Integration (Recent): Most recently, the C.U.N.T.S. recruited HighLord, an active-duty veteran of the Helldivers Programme. Super Earth has formally requested his return on three separate occasions. All three requests arrived via orbital drop pod — which is, apparently, how Super Earth does diplomacy. Two of the pods landed in the car park. The third destroyed floor 14 of UNSC headquarters. The floor contained the original copies of six diplomatic communiqués asking Super Earth to please stop sending things via orbital drop pod. The irony was noted. Relations with Super Earth are described in official documentation as "complicated," which is the diplomatic word for "we are afraid of them."
